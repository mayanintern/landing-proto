What makes Mayan different
The market competes for your swap
Every swap kicks off a live auction. Independent drivers compete to fill your order at the best rate they can offer — and the highest bid is what you receive. The market works for you, automatically.

What you see is what you get
Most bridges quote an estimate and deliver something less. Mayan locks the output amount before execution — the number shown is the number that arrives, every time.

One swap, any destination action
A swap used to be step one. Zaps makes it the only step. Builders attach a destination action — deposit, stake, any contract call. Users submit one intent. Both complete together.
